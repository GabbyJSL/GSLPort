Hey! I'm Gabriel.

Amateur software, web and unity game developer.

Hi! I'm Gabriel a 24 year old Environmental Science graduate currently based in the UK trying to break into the world of software, web and game design!

After graduating with a 2:1 BSc Environmental Science from Sheffield Hallam University in 2017, I went onto working a summer in the US for Universal Studios Orlando Florida, after returning to the UK I delved into online courses in Maya, Blender, Unity and Unreal Engine hungry for knowledge with a desire to create I flung myself into coding with relation to C# in Unity and began to branch out with Html5, CSS, Python and Javascript.

After experimenting in my free time, unlocking milestones and having completed my first projects, I decided to make the first steps towards building a career as a developer by combinding my passions of game and software design, my academic background with spatial data packages to look towards gathering professional experience as an apprentice while continuing to learn autonomously.
